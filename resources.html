CRISIS HOTLINE: 281 - CARE
Help is a phone call away.

Dial 281-CARE or (513) 281-2273 if you are in need of immediate assistance.

Since 1970, this 24-hour suicide and crisis hotline service has been available to the community. This service also provides prevention, assessment, and educational programming to individuals of any age. Short-term counseling is also available for adults. Staff members can assist with stress management, relationship conflicts and coping with a loss. Services are free, confidential, and TTY-accessible
